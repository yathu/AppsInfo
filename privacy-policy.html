Privacy Policy – QuickScan – Document Scanner

Effective Date: March 30, 2026

QuickScan – Document Scanner ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

  • No account registration is required
  • No login or signup
  • No personal data collection (name, email, phone number, etc.)

All document scanning and processing happens locally on your device.

2. Camera Usage

The App requires access to your device camera to scan documents.

  • The camera is used only for scanning purposes
  • No images or data are sent to any server
  • All scanned files are stored locally on your device

3. File Access

The App may request storage permission to:

  • Save scanned PDF files
  • Allow you to view and manage your documents

All files remain on your device and are not uploaded or shared without your action.

4. Ads (Google AdMob)

In future versions, the App may display ads provided by Google AdMob.

  • Google may collect limited data for ad personalization and performance
  • This may include device information and usage data

For more information, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data, your data remains secure on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
